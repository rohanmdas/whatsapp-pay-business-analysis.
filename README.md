# WhatsApp Pay Business Analysis

This repository contains a business analysis case study evaluating the launch of WhatsApp Pay in India. The project analyzes the digital payments ecosystem, identifies key stakeholders, and defines the functional and non-functional requirements required to support a scalable UPI-based payment solution.

The analysis also includes process workflow design, risk considerations, and strategic recommendations to support adoption of WhatsApp Pay in a competitive fintech market.

## Objectives

* Evaluate the feasibility of launching WhatsApp Pay within the Indian digital payments ecosystem
* Identify internal and external stakeholders involved in the payment platform
* Design current-state and future-state process workflows for peer-to-peer payments
* Define functional and non-functional system requirements
* Provide strategic recommendations to support adoption and scalability

## Key Business Goals

* Achieve **1 million users within the first 3 months** of launch
* Reach **30% user adoption within the first year**
* Ensure scalability to support **up to 400 million users**

## Analysis Methods

* Stakeholder Analysis
* SWOT Analysis
* Process Flow Mapping
* Requirement Analysis

## Repository Purpose

This repository demonstrates structured business analysis and product thinking applied to a fintech payment solution. It is intended as a portfolio project illustrating capabilities in stakeholder analysis, process design, and requirements definition.
